The best movie I have ever seen - Top 3

The Notebook

A touching love story read by an elderly man from an old notebook to a woman in a nursing home. This is the story of the relationship between a young man and a girl from different social strata who lived in North Carolina. Noah and Ellie spent an unforgettable summer together, until they were separated first by their parents, and then by World War II.

Divergent

The action takes place on Earth in the future, in despotic Chicago. All teenagers over the age of 16 are required to choose one of the five factions and join it for the rest of their lives. Each faction represents a certain quality: Sincerity, Renunciation, Fearlessness, Friendliness and Erudition.

Interstellar

Our time on Earth has come to an end, a team of researchers is taking on the most important mission in human history; traveling beyond our galaxy to find out if humanity has a future among the stars.
